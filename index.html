The story

Space to be
entirely yourself.

FADAA was born from a simple belief: the things we carry should be made slowly, honestly, and with intention. Every bag is crocheted by hand — no two stitches quite the same — so what you hold is genuinely one of a kind.

We design beyond the binary. A FADAA bag belongs on any shoulder, any arm, any adventure — for him, for her, for everyone in between.

  • 01 Natural, responsibly sourced yarns
  • 02 Made by independent artisans
  • 03 Designed to last, made to be loved
